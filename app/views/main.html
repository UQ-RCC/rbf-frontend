Welcome to the Robin Boyd Foundation’s digital archive.

The database explores the breadth of Robin Boyd’s architectural career. It comprises his built and unbuilt projects, and hence, it serves as a portal to audiovisual and textual files that allow you to explore relationships between people, projects and firms.

This archive compliments our Walsh Street House Collection, which contains over 4000 objects from our house museum and Robin Boyd’s personal archive, that have been researched, digitised and uploaded to the Victorian Collection platform at: Robin Boyd House

Learn more about Robin Boyd and our Foundation at: Robinboyd.org.au

Make sure to also test the research tools to build your customised map, timeline, or word cloud and gain a deeper understanding of Queensland's architectural history.

We acknowledge the support of the University of Queensland’s faculty of architecture in developing and sharing this platform with us. Their work on this project dates back to 2014 and is ongoing. To explore their database of Queensland Architecture, visit: Digital Archive of Queensland Architecture
